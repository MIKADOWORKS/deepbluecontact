## 2. About This Game / ゲーム紹介文

### English

**Below 200 meters, sunlight disappears. Below 1,000 meters, the world goes silent. Below 6,000 meters, fewer humans have visited than the surface of the Moon.**

Deep Blue Contact is a deep-sea exploration simulator where you send submersibles into the ocean's darkest reaches and await their return. Each expedition brings back photographs of real creatures that live in perpetual darkness -- documented with authentic imagery from NOAA Ocean Exploration and JAMSTEC.

This is not a survival game. There are no monsters to fight, no oxygen meters ticking down. Just the slow descent of a submersible, the glow of bioluminescence, and the quiet thrill of seeing something no one expected to find.

**Key Features**

- **100 Real Deep-Sea Species** -- Every creature in your encyclopedia is a real animal, presented in authentic photographs sourced from NOAA and JAMSTEC research archives. No illustrations. No artistic interpretations. The real thing.

- **Watch or Wait -- Your Choice** -- Enter Observation Mode to follow your submersible's descent in real time (with 2x speed option), or send it off and come back later. Both paths lead to discovery.

- **No Microtransactions. Ever.** -- Everything is unlocked through gameplay. No premium currency, no timers you can pay to skip, no season passes. One purchase. The full deep sea.

- **A Documentary, Not a Horror Film** -- The tone is calm, curious, and grounded. Think nature documentary, not creature feature. The deep ocean is strange enough without adding fiction.

- **Build Your Encyclopedia** -- Every species you discover is logged with scientific detail. Find them again to unlock additional entries with real biological facts and trivia.

- **Upgrade Your Fleet** -- Choose from multiple submersible classes, each suited to different depths. Equip sonar arrays, reinforced hulls, lighting rigs, and sampling tools to push deeper.

- **Solo Development, Honest Craft** -- Made by one person at MIKADO WORKS. Every design choice serves the experience, not a revenue model.

**How It Works**

Your expedition follows three steps:

1. **Prepare** -- Select a depth zone, choose your submersible, and equip it with parts across four categories.
2. **Explore** -- Launch the dive. Watch the descent in Observation Mode or let the expedition run in the background.
3. **Return** -- Review what your submersible found. New species are logged in your encyclopedia. Record-breaking specimens are noted. Experience is earned, opening the path to deeper waters.

One submersible at a time. One expedition at a time. Simple by design.

**Four Depth Zones**

| Zone | Depth | Idle Time | Observation Mode (2x) | Species |
|------|-------|-----------|----------------------|---------|
| Mesopelagic | 200 -- 1,000m | ~20 min | ~10 min | 35 |
| Bathypelagic | 1,000 -- 3,000m | ~1 hour | ~30 min | 30 |
| Abyssopelagic | 3,000 -- 6,000m | ~3 hours | ~1.5 hours | 22 |
| Hadal | 6,000m+ | ~6 hours | ~3 hours | 13 |

The deeper you go, the longer the expedition -- and the rarer the creatures you may encounter.

**Early Access Roadmap**

Deep Blue Contact is in Early Access. The current build includes the full core loop -- preparation, exploration, and encyclopedia -- with the first set of deep-sea species across all four depth zones. During Early Access, we plan to expand the species roster, add new submersible types and equipment, and refine the experience based on community feedback. Your voice shapes the depths we explore next.

Deep Blue Contactは、深海探査艇を暗黒の海へ送り出し、帰還を待つ深海探査シミュレーターです。探査艇が持ち帰るのは、永遠の闇に棲む本物の生物の記録。NOAA Ocean ExplorationとJAMSTECの実写映像で撮影された、深海のリアルな姿がそこにあります。

サバイバルゲームではありません。戦う敵もいなければ、減り続ける酸素ゲージもありません。あるのは、静かに沈んでいく探査艇、生物発光のかすかな光、そして予想もしなかった生物との出会いがもたらす静かな興奮だけです。

**主な特徴**

- **実写で収録された100種の深海生物** -- 図鑑に登録されるすべての生物は実在する動物であり、NOAAとJAMSTECの研究アーカイブから提供された実写写真で表示されます。イラストでも想像図でもない、本物の深海の姿です。

- **眺めても楽しい、放置してもOK** -- 観察モードで探査艇の潜行をリアルタイムに追う（2倍速あり）こともできますし、送り出して後から結果を確認することもできます。どちらの遊び方でも発見は訪れます。

- **課金要素は一切なし** -- すべてがプレイで解放されます。プレミアム通貨も、時短課金も、シーズンパスもありません。買い切りひとつで、深海のすべてが手に入ります。

- **ホラーではなく、ドキュメンタリー** -- トーンは穏やかで、知的好奇心に満ちています。ネイチャードキュメンタリーのような体験です。深海は、フィクションを加えなくても十分に不思議な世界です。

- **探査艇と装備を強化** -- 異なる深度に適した複数の探査艇から選択し、ソナー、強化外殻、照明装置、サンプリングツールの4カテゴリの装備で深海への到達力を高めましょう。

- **個人開発、誠実なものづくり** -- MIKADO WORKSによる個人開発です。すべてのデザイン判断は、収益モデルではなく体験の質のために行われています。

**遊び方**

探査は3つのステップで進みます。

1. **準備** -- 深度帯を選び、探査艇を選択し、4カテゴリの装備を搭載します。
2. **探査** -- 出発。観察モードで潜行を見届けるか、バックグラウンドで探査を進めます。
3. **帰還** -- 探査結果を確認。新種は図鑑に登録され、最大サイズの記録が更新されます。経験値を獲得し、より深い海域への道が開かれます。

同時に出せる探査艇は1隻だけ。「今この1回の探査」に集中する、シンプルな設計です。

| 中深層 | 200 -- 1,000m | 約20分 | 約10分 | 35種 |
| 漸深層 | 1,000 -- 3,000m | 約1時間 | 約30分 | 30種 |

深く潜るほど探査時間は長くなり、より希少な生物と出会える可能性が高まります。

## 3. Early Access Description / アーリーアクセス説明文

### English

**Why Early Access?**

Deep Blue Contact is a solo-developed project by MIKADO WORKS. Early Access allows us to build the game alongside a community of players who share a genuine curiosity about the deep ocean. Your feedback directly shapes which species we add, how expeditions feel, and what features matter most.

**Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?**

We anticipate approximately 6 to 12 months of Early Access before the full release. The timeline depends on the scope of additions guided by community feedback.

**How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?**

The full version will include:
- The complete roster of 100 deep-sea species with full encyclopedia entries
- All planned submersible classes and equipment tiers
- Polished UI, sound design, and quality-of-life improvements
- Full English and Japanese localization
- Additional content shaped by Early Access feedback

**What is the current state of the Early Access version?**

The core gameplay loop is fully playable:
- Prepare expeditions by selecting depth zones, submersibles, and equipment
- Explore via Observation Mode or idle play
- Discover and catalog real deep-sea species in your encyclopedia
- Upgrade submersibles and unlock deeper zones
- All four depth zones (200m to 6,000m+) are accessible

**Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?**

The Early Access price will be lower than the full release price. Players who purchase during Early Access receive all future updates at no additional cost. We will announce any price changes well in advance.

**How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?**

We actively read and respond to Steam Community discussions and reviews. We also maintain a Discord server where players can suggest species, report issues, vote on feature priorities, and share their expedition stories. Major development decisions will be shared transparently through devlog updates.
